Misiunea-web

Misiunea-web.com is my first attempt at blogging. It started out as a free Wordpress blog and as I got accustomed with blogging I registered a domain and host to manage Wordpress on my own. It soon became a place of experiments as I began to try different Wordpress frameworks, features, plugins or just play with php.

Wordpress is an awesome CMS but the desire for control lead me to Jekyll, the static site generator. With Jekyll I got to work hands on with templating, SASS and Github.

On this blog I write about web and design and hope that someday others will join. It is a portal of self assessment and a place to try new web technologies, writing along as I experience them.
